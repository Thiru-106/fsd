FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes ŌĆö upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect on your next billing date.

Is there a fee on freelancer payments?

A small 5% platform fee applies to all transactions on Free & Pro. Enterprise plans can negotiate custom rates with our sales team.

What payment methods are accepted?

UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), net banking, and bank transfers. Enterprise clients may also pay via invoice.

Do I need a card to sign up?

No. The Free plan needs no payment info at all. Add a payment method only when you're ready to hire or upgrade.
